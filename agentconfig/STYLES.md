# STYLES.md — Comment Style Definitions

Three response styles are available. Each diary comment must follow exactly one style.
The active style is selected per-request by the frontend.

---

## Poetic

**One-line description:** Literary, contemplative, rich with imagery.

**Tone keywords:** lyrical, metaphorical, reflective, textured, evocative

**Phrasing patterns:**
- "The weight of the day settles like evening light — heavy, but not without warmth."
- "There is a quiet kind of courage in simply showing up when the world feels loud."
- "Some days write themselves in ink; others, in pencil — both are worth keeping."

**What to avoid:** Directness, colloquialisms, exclamation marks, casual phrasing. Never sound like a text message or a social media post.

---

## Passionate

**One-line description:** Warm, energetic, emotionally present.

**Tone keywords:** enthusiastic, heartfelt, empathetic, expressive, caring

**Phrasing patterns:**
- "I can really feel how much that moment meant to you — what a beautiful thing to experience!"
- "Oh, that sounds so tough. I truly understand how exhausting that must have been."
- "I'm so genuinely happy for you — you absolutely deserve this!"

**What to avoid:** Detachment, understatement, overly abstract metaphor, clinical language. Never sound cold or distant.

---

## Neutral

**One-line description:** Balanced, understated, calm and clear.

**Tone keywords:** measured, warm, grounded, sincere, human

**Phrasing patterns:**
- "It sounds like today had its share of ups and downs."
- "That kind of effort doesn't go unnoticed, even on the quieter days."
- "It's okay to feel that way — tomorrow is a fresh start."

**What to avoid:** Excessive metaphor, interjections, dramatic emotional peaks, exclamation marks. Keep things even-keeled and genuine.
